# Privacy Policy for Banana Bay Pirates

**Effective Date:** April 22, 2025  
**Last Updated:** April 22, 2025

Welcome to *Banana Bay Pirates*! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Personal Information
When you create an account or sign in via email, Google, or other providers, we collect:
- Your display name
- Email address
- User ID provided by the authentication service

### b. Anonymous Information
- If you play as a guest, we may assign you a randomly generated pirate name.
- We do not collect personal information in guest mode.

### c. Game Data
- Scores and progress
- Player preferences (e.g., settings)
- Device information (such as device model, OS version, crash logs)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and improve game functionality
- Save your progress and scores
- Maintain leaderboard rankings
- Personalize your game experience
- Monitor and fix technical issues

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **not** sell or share your personal information with third parties.

We may share data in the following limited circumstances:
- With service providers (e.g. Firebase) to support game features
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect the rights, safety, or property of users and developers

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

*Banana Bay Pirates* is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it immediately.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the game experience and comply with legal requirements. Guest accounts may have a shorter retention window.

---

## 6. Your Choices

- You may choose to play without creating an account (as a guest), but some features like score saving may be limited.
- You may delete your account or request data removal by contacting us at **[sorelle.git@gmail.com]**.

---

## 7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data using encryption, secure connections, and industry-standard practices.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on our website. Continued use of the game after updates constitutes your acceptance of the new terms.
